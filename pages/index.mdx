# Introduction

Welcome to epitech-docs.reubanbryenton.com! This is a basic survival guide to epitech from a student at epitech TLS.

## What can this site teach you?

This site was made to teach first year students the basics of how epitech works, what is expected of them and also to give some tips on how to survive the year.
